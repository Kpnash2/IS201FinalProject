Disc Golf

Health Benefits

There are many reasons to get involved with Disc Golfing. The sport encourages walking outdoors while participating in varying difficulty of shot types. Usually disc golf courses have up to 18 holes. On average, a player will spend about 1 hour walking over 2 miles. This can be up hills, through trees, and more scenic views! All benefit from getting outside, seeing amazing views, and spending time with loved ones. Therefore, the health benefits experienced by all who participate are immense.

Varying Levels of Difficulty

One amazing aspect of disc golf is that it is absolutely amazing for individuals of all skill levels! There are different courses designed for different difficulties. Additionally, some courses have different tee boxes making the distance to throw vary depending on the difficulty you want! This makes disc golf the perfect activity whether you want to have a casual date night or if you want to have an intense tournament. There are a few ways to vary the level of difficulty of a disc golf course: obstacles like trees, water, or large hills, distance to throw, weather conditions, and more! No matter what you may be looking for-disc golf has the solution for you!

Skills to Master

Another amazing aspect of disc golf is that there are many difficult skills or shot types to master that keep the activity exciting and engaging. These include:
  1. Shot Shapes
    • Flex Shot
    • S-Shot
    • Anhyzer
  2. Ways to Throw Different Kinds of Discs
    • Drivers
    • Mid-Rangers
    • Putters
  3. Creativity with Different Courses
  4. Form on Different Throws
    • Form for Putting
    • Form for Driving
